# Expense Management and Budget Procedures

## Expense Reporting
### Submitting Expenses
- Use Concur expense management system
- Submit within 30 days of incurring expense
- Original receipts required for purchases >$25
- Manager approval required for all submissions
- Processing time: 3-5 business days

### Reimbursable Expenses
- Business travel (flights, hotels, meals)
- Client entertainment (with prior approval)
- Home office equipment (up to $500 annually)
- Professional development and training
- Business mileage ($0.65 per mile)

### Non-Reimbursable Expenses
- Personal entertainment or meals
- Commuting costs to regular office
- Late fees or penalties
- Alcohol during business meals
- Personal development without business justification

### Credit Card Usage
- Company credit cards available for frequent business expenses
- Monthly statements must be reconciled by 5th of each month
- Personal charges not allowed
- Credit limit: $5,000 (may be increased with manager approval)
- Lost cards reported immediately to Finance department

## Budget Management
### Department Budgets
- Annual budgets approved by December 1st for next year
- Quarterly budget reviews conducted
- Budget reallocations require VP approval
- Over-budget requests need business case justification
- Unused budget does not carry over to next year

### Capital Expenditures
- CapEx requests >$10,000 require CFO approval
- ROI analysis required for all major purchases
- Depreciation schedules: 3-5 years for equipment
- Asset tagging and inventory management required
- Disposal approval required for all company assets

### Software and Subscriptions
- Annual subscription review conducted in Q3
- Department heads approve recurring subscriptions
- Enterprise agreements available for common software
- Software usage monitored for optimization
- Termination notice: 30 days for monthly, 90 days for annual contracts

## Procurement Processes
### Purchase Orders
- POs required for purchases >$1,000
- Three vendor quotes required for purchases >$5,000
- Standard payment terms: Net 30 days
- Early payment discounts: 2% for payment within 10 days
- Emergency purchases: verbal approval required within 24 hours

### Vendor Management
- Preferred vendor program with negotiated rates
- Vendor performance reviews conducted quarterly
- New vendor onboarding takes 2-3 weeks
- Insurance requirements: $1M general liability
- Background checks required for service providers

### Contract Management
- Legal review required for contracts >$25,000
- Standard contract templates available for common services
- Contract renewal reminders sent 90 days before expiration
- Payment terms negotiation encouraged for long-term contracts
- All contracts stored in centralized contract repository

## Financial Controls
### Approval Authority
- Manager approval: up to $1,000
- Director approval: $1,001 - $5,000
- VP approval: $5,001 - $25,000
- CFO approval: $25,001 - $100,000
- CEO/Board approval: >$100,000

### Audit Requirements
- Internal audits conducted quarterly
- External audit performed annually
- Expense random audits: 10% of submissions reviewed
- Documentation retention: 7 years for all financial records
- Segregation of duties enforced for sensitive transactions

### Compliance and Reporting
- Monthly financial reports to department heads
- Quarterly business reviews with executive team
- Annual budget presentations to Board of Directors
- Tax compliance reviewed by external CPA firm
- Regulatory filings managed by Finance team

## Employee Financial Benefits
### Salary Advances
- Available to employees with 1+ year tenure
- Maximum advance: 50% of one month's salary
- Repayment through payroll deductions (maximum 6 months)
- No interest charged for salary advances
- Processing time: 2-3 business days after approval

### Tuition Reimbursement
- Maximum annual reimbursement: $5,250
- Courses must be job-related or career advancement
- Grade requirement: B or higher for undergraduate courses
- Approval required before course enrollment
- Reimbursement paid upon successful course completion

### Relocation Assistance
- Available for new hires and internal transfers >50 miles
- Maximum benefit: $10,000
- Covers moving expenses, temporary housing, travel
- Repayment required if employment ends within 12 months
- Tax implications explained during approval process
