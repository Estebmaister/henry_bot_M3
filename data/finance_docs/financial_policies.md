# Company Financial Policies and Procedures

## Payroll and Compensation
### Pay Schedule
- Bi-weekly payroll: every other Friday
- Direct deposit mandatory for all employees
- Pay stubs available through employee self-service portal
- Time sheet submission deadline: Monday 5 PM for previous week
- Overtime approval required before hours worked

### Salary Structure
- Annual salary reviews conducted in December
- Merit increases based on performance and market analysis
- Promotions accompanied by minimum 10% salary increase
- Salary bands maintained for each job level
- Geographic differentials applied for high-cost areas

### Bonus and Incentives
- Annual performance bonus paid in March
- Target bonus: 10-20% of base salary (varies by level)
- Spot bonuses available for exceptional contributions
- Referral bonuses: $2,000 for successful hires
- Profit sharing participation for employees with 2+ years tenure

## Tax and Compliance
### Tax Withholding
- Federal, state, and local taxes withheld per regulations
- W-2 forms distributed by January 31st
- W-4 forms must be current and on file
- Additional withholding allowed upon employee request
- Tax withholding calculator available on HR portal

### Benefits Taxation
- Health insurance premiums: pre-tax deduction
- 401(k) contributions: pre-tax up to annual limit
- Health savings account (HSA): tax-free contributions
- Transportation benefits: pre-tax up to IRS limits
- Educational assistance: tax-free up to $5,250 annually

### Reporting Requirements
- Form 1099 issued to contractors earning >$600
- Quarterly tax filings for payroll taxes
- Annual reports filed with state agencies
- Workers compensation audits conducted annually
- Unemployment insurance rates reviewed yearly

## Financial Planning Resources
### Employee Assistance Program
- Free financial counseling sessions available
- Retirement planning guidance provided
- Debt management assistance offered
- Budget planning tools and workshops
- Emergency financial assistance available

### Retirement Planning
- 401(k) plan with company matching (100% up to 6%)
- Roth 401(k) option available
- Financial advisor consultations quarterly
- Retirement planning workshops semi-annually
- Catch-up contributions allowed for employees 50+

### Insurance Benefits
- Life insurance: 1x annual salary (company-paid)
- Additional life insurance available at group rates
- Disability insurance: short-term and long-term coverage
- Critical illness insurance available as voluntary benefit
- Accident insurance coverage for medical expenses

## Financial Systems Access
### Payroll System
- ADP Workforce Now for payroll and time tracking
- Mobile app available for time off requests
- Self-service features for tax document access
- Pay history available for past 3 years
- Direct deposit changes processed within 2 pay periods

### Expense Management
- Concur solution for expense reporting and reimbursement
- Mobile app for receipt scanning and submission
- Corporate card integration for automatic expense import
- Expense policy enforcement with real-time validation
- Approval workflows configurable by department

### Budget Tracking
- Adaptive Insights for budget planning and tracking
- Real-time budget vs. actual reporting
- Forecasting tools for annual budget preparation
- Department-level access with role-based permissions
- Automated alerts for budget threshold breaches

## Financial Security Measures
### Data Protection
- Financial data encrypted at rest and in transit
- Multi-factor authentication required for system access
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Employee financial data access restricted to authorized personnel
- Secure document storage with audit trails

### Fraud Prevention
- Segregation of duties enforced in financial processes
- Mandatory vacation policy for finance team members
- Regular internal audits of financial transactions
- Anonymous fraud reporting hotline available
- Background checks conducted for finance department hires

### Business Continuity
- Daily backups of financial systems
- Disaster recovery plan tested quarterly
- Alternative processing sites for emergency operations
- Cross-training of critical finance functions
- Insurance coverage for business interruption
