# Employee Benefits Guide

## Health Insurance
We offer comprehensive health insurance plans through multiple providers:
- Medical, dental, and vision coverage
- Employer contribution covers 80% of premium costs
- New employees can enroll within 30 days of hire date
- Open enrollment period: November 1-30 each year

## Time Off Policies
### Paid Time Off (PTO)
- Full-time employees receive 20 days PTO annually
- Accrues at 1.67 days per month
- Can carry over up to 10 days to next year
- Minimum 30 days notice for extended leave

### Holidays
- 10 paid federal holidays per year
- Floating holidays: 2 additional days per year
- Holiday schedule published by January 1st

## Remote Work Policy
### Eligibility
- Employees with 6+ months tenure
- Roles that can be performed remotely
- Manager approval required

### Requirements
- High-speed internet connection (25+ Mbps)
- Dedicated workspace with ergonomic furniture
- Company-provided laptop and security software
- Weekly virtual team meetings attendance

## Salary and Compensation
### Pay Schedule
- Bi-weekly pay schedule (every other Friday)
- Direct deposit required
- Pay stubs available through employee portal

### Performance Reviews
- Annual performance reviews in December
- Mid-year check-ins in June
- Salary increases based on performance and market rates

## Retirement Benefits
### 401(k) Plan
- Employer matching: 100% up to 6% of salary
- Immediate vesting for employer contributions
- Multiple investment options available
- Enrollment available year-round
