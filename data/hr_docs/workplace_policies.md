# Workplace Policies and Procedures

## Code of Conduct
### Professional Behavior
- Respect all colleagues regardless of position or background
- Maintain confidentiality of sensitive company information
- Report harassment or discrimination to HR immediately
- Zero tolerance for workplace violence or threats

### Communication Guidelines
- Professional email etiquette required
- Response time expectations: 24 hours for internal emails
- Use company-approved communication channels
- No personal social media during work hours

## Dress Code
### Business Casual (Most Offices)
- Slacks, khakis, or dress pants
- Button-down shirts, blouses, or sweaters
- Closed-toe shoes required
- No jeans, t-shirts, or athletic wear

### Business Professional (Client Meetings)
- Suits or sport coats for men
- Business suits or conservative dresses for women
- Professional footwear required
- Grooming standards maintained

## Training and Development
### Required Training
- New hire orientation (first week)
- Annual security awareness training
- Anti-harassment training (biennial)
- Role-specific compliance training

### Professional Development
- $2,000 annual training budget per employee
- Tuition reimbursement for job-related courses
- Conference attendance with manager approval
- Internal skill-sharing sessions monthly

## Workplace Safety
### Emergency Procedures
- Fire exits clearly marked throughout building
- Monthly fire drills conducted
- First aid stations in common areas
- Emergency contact list posted prominently

### Ergonomics
- Adjustable chairs and desks provided
- Monitor stands and keyboard trays available
- Ergonomic assessments upon request
- Break reminders every 2 hours

## Attendance and Punctuality
### Work Hours
- Standard hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Monday-Friday
- Flex-time available with manager approval
- Core hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM
- Lunch break: 30-60 minutes

### Absence Reporting
- Notify manager at least 2 hours before start time
- Use company absence reporting system
- Medical documentation required after 3 consecutive days
- Planned absences require 2 weeks notice
